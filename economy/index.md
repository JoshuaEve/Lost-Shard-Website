# Economy

LostShard uses **gold** as its primary currency. Gold is earned through gameplay and spent on skill training, plot creation, clan operations, and more.

## Gold

Gold is the universal currency for all transactions on LostShard. Your current balance is always visible in `/stats`.

### Earning Gold

Gold is earned through:

- Killing enemies and players
- Completing in-world activities
- Trading with other players
- Server events

### Spending Gold

| Purchase | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Skill training (most skills) | 4 gold per level |
| Fishing training | 30 gold per level |
| Blacksmithy training | 40 gold per level |
| Plot creation | 1000 gold + 1 diamond |
| Plot upgrade to Town | 135,000 gold |
| Plot upgrade to Dungeon | 40,000 gold |
| Plot upgrade to Neutral | 10,000 gold |
| Plot AutoKick feature | 25,000 gold |
| Clan rename | 1,000 gold |

## Skill Training Costs

Skills can be trained at NPC trainers up to **level 250**. Training costs vary by skill:

| Skill | Cost per Level | Max Trained Level |
|-------|---------------|------------------|
| Blades | 4 gold | 250 |
| Archery | 4 gold | 250 |
| Brawling | 4 gold | 250 |
| Mining | 4 gold | 250 |
| Lumberjacking | 4 gold | 250 |
| Fishing | 30 gold | 250 |
| Blacksmithy | 40 gold | 250 |

::: tip
Training from 0 to 250 in Fishing costs **7,500 gold**. Training Blacksmithy from 0 to 250 costs **10,000 gold**. Level naturally as far as you can before paying for training to minimize cost.
:::

## Plot Treasury

Each plot has its own **treasury** (money pool). Clan members can deposit gold into a plot's treasury and withdraw from it. Plot taxes are deducted from the treasury automatically.

## Bank

Your personal **bank** is separate from your gold balance — it is a 27-slot item storage that persists across sessions. It cannot be used to store gold directly, only items.

```
/bank
```
