# Taming

**Taming** is the utility skill that lets you build a pack of tamed wolves. Higher Taming skill increases the number of pets you can have active, and you can summon and command your entire pack from the `/pets` menu.

## Taming Wolves

Right-click on a wolf while holding **bones** to begin taming. Each bone has a chance to tame the wolf. Once tamed, the wolf is added to your roster and tracked by name.

## Pet Management

Open the pet menu with:

```
/pets
```

From here you can:

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| View roster | See all your tamed wolves |
| Summon pet | Call a specific wolf to your location — costs **20 stamina** |
| Summon all | Summon your entire pack — costs **20 stamina** |
| Release pet | Permanently release a wolf from your roster |

::: info
Summoning pets costs **20 stamina**. Make sure you have enough stamina before calling your pack into battle.
:::

## Pet Capacity

The maximum number of pets you can have active at once scales with your Taming skill level. A higher Taming skill means a bigger pack.

## Persistence

All pet data — wolf names, UUIDs, and ownership — is stored in the database and persists across server restarts. Your pets will always be there when you come back.

## Rebirth: Alpha Bond

Once Taming reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Taming to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Alpha Bond** passive, which strengthens your connection with your pack.

## Tips

- Taming works well with [Archery](/skills/archery) — your wolves engage melee while you shoot from range.
- The [Mining](/skills/mining) stamina bonus complements Taming's stamina costs, letting you summon more often.
- Keep pets summoned during exploration — wolves provide valuable backup against hostile mobs.
