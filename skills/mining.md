# Mining

**Mining** is the primary resource-gathering skill. Breaking ore blocks with a pickaxe grants Mining skill points, and at higher levels you gain a chance at bonus loot drops — including rare materials like diamonds and gold ingots.

## Leveling Mining

Mine ore blocks with a pickaxe to gain skill points. Every ore block broken awards experience.

## Bonus Stamina

At high Mining skill, your maximum stamina is increased as a reward for your time underground:

- **Max bonus:** +10 stamina at very high skill levels

## Bonus Loot Table

High Mining skill unlocks a chance to receive additional drops from ore blocks:

| Bonus Drop | Min Skill Required | Drop Chance |
|------------|-------------------|------------|
| Coal | 0 | Always available |
| Redstone | 0 | ~0% base (scales up) |
| Iron Ingot | 250 | 0.5% |
| Diamond | 500 | 0.25% |
| Emerald | 750 | 0.25% |
| Gold Ingot | 1000 | 0.1% |

::: tip
These drops are **bonus** items on top of normal ore drops — they do not replace the standard loot.
:::

## Training

Mining can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 4 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

## Rebirth: Deep Delver

Once Mining reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Mining to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Deep Delver** passive.

## Tips

- Focus on diamond-level ores when pushing past 500 to maximise gold value from bonus drops.
- The stamina bonus at high skill is useful for [Blacksmithy](/skills/blacksmithy) and [Taming](/skills/taming) operations.
- Mining and [Blacksmithy](/skills/blacksmithy) naturally pair well — mine your own materials and then craft with them.
