# Lumberjacking

**Lumberjacking** is the woodcutting skill. Breaking log blocks with an axe grants skill points. At higher levels you chop more efficiently, with less fatigue between cuts.

## Leveling Lumberjacking

Break log blocks while holding an axe to gain Lumberjacking skill points.

## Exhaustion Mechanic

Lumberjacking has a built-in **exhaustion (cooldown)** system between cuts. At lower skill levels the cooldown is longer; as your skill increases the fatigue decreases, letting you chop trees faster.

## Training

Lumberjacking can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 4 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

## Rebirth: Old Growth

Once Lumberjacking reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Lumberjacking to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Old Growth** passive.

## Tips

- Chopping large trees in quick succession is the fastest way to level Lumberjacking.
- The reduced exhaustion at high levels makes resource gathering significantly faster.
- Pair with [Survivalism](/skills/survivalism) if you're building camps, since both rely on wood-related activities.
