# Skills

LostShard has **10 skills** that cover every aspect of gameplay — fighting, gathering, crafting, magic, and more. Skills are leveled through natural use and can be accelerated via paid training at NPC trainers.

## How Skills Work

### Gaining Skill Points

Each skill levels up by performing its associated action:

- Swing a sword → gain **Blades**
- Mine ore → gain **Mining**
- Cast spells → gain **Magery**
- Tame wolves → gain **Taming**

Skill points are awarded passively as you play. You don't need to do anything special.

### Skill Cap & Training

| Phase | Points | How to Reach |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| Natural play | 0 – 250 | Gained through use |
| Trainer training | 250 (max trained) | Pay gold at a trainer NPC |
| Full cap | 1000 | Continued use past 250 |

::: info
Training at an NPC costs **gold** and increases your cap up to 250. Past 250, skills continue to climb through use alone.
:::

### Damage Tiers (Combat Skills)

Combat skills (Blades, Archery, Brawling) unlock bonus damage at milestone thresholds:

| Skill Range | Bonus Damage Multiplier |
|-------------|------------------------|
| 0 – 249 | +0 |
| 250 – 499 | +1× |
| 500 – 749 | +2× |
| 750 – 999 | +3× |
| 1000 | +4× |

### Rebirths

At max skill level, you can **rebirth** a skill — resetting it to 0 in exchange for a permanent perk unique to that skill. Each rebirth unlocks a named passive ability.

## Viewing Your Skills

Open the skill menu at any time with:

```
/skills
```

Click a skill to see its current level, progress, available training options, and rebirth status.
