# Blades

The **Blades** skill (also called Swordsmanship) governs your effectiveness with swords and bladed weapons. Higher Blades skill adds bonus damage to every sword hit and eventually unlocks a powerful **Bleed** debuff.

## Leveling Blades

Blades experience is gained by hitting entities (players or mobs) with a sword. The more you fight, the faster the skill grows.

## Damage Bonus

Blades adds flat bonus damage to sword attacks based on skill tier:

| Skill Range | Bonus Damage |
|-------------|-------------|
| 0 – 249 | +0 |
| 250 – 499 | +1 |
| 500 – 749 | +2 |
| 750 – 999 | +3 |
| 1000 | +4 |

## Bleed

At high Blades skill, your sword strikes can apply a **Bleed** debuff to your target. Bleed deals damage over time for several seconds.

::: info
The exact Bleed proc chance and damage scale with your Blades level. Keep your skill high to maximize the chance of inflicting it in combat.
:::

## Training

Blades can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 4 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

Past 250, the skill continues to grow naturally through combat.

## Rebirth: Crimson Edge

Once Blades reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Blades to 0 and unlocks the **Crimson Edge** passive, granting a permanent bonus that carries through all future playthroughs.

## Tips

- Sword type matters for base damage, but the Blades skill bonus stacks on top.
- Reaching 250 before paying for training is the most efficient path.
- Pair high Blades with [Magery](/skills/magery) spells for devastating combo potential.
