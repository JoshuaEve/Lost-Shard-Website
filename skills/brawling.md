# Brawling

**Brawling** is the unarmed combat skill. If you prefer to fight without a weapon, Brawling increases the damage you deal with bare fists and rewards you with the **Iron Knuckles** rebirth perk.

## Leveling Brawling

Brawling experience is gained by punching entities while unarmed (empty main hand). Every punch that connects awards skill points.

## Damage Bonus

Brawling adds bonus damage to unarmed attacks based on skill tier:

| Skill Range | Bonus Damage |
|-------------|-------------|
| 0 – 249 | +0 |
| 250 – 499 | +1 |
| 500 – 749 | +2 |
| 750 – 999 | +3 |
| 1000 | +4 |

## Training

Brawling can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 4 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

## Rebirth: Iron Knuckles

Once Brawling reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Brawling to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Iron Knuckles** passive.

## Tips

- Brawling is a high-risk style — unarmed fighters get close and stay there.
- Supplement with [Taming](/skills/taming) pets to take pressure off you in fights.
- The full 4× damage bonus at 1000 makes unarmed combat a surprisingly powerful choice for dedicated brawlers.
