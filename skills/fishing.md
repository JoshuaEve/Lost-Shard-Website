# Fishing

**Fishing** is the aquatic gathering skill. Use a fishing rod to gain skill points and unlock better catches over time. Fishing has a higher training cost than most skills, reflecting its passive playstyle.

## Leveling Fishing

Cast a fishing rod and catch fish to gain skill points. Every successful catch awards experience.

## Training

Fishing can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 30 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

::: warning
Fishing training costs **30 gold per level** — significantly more than most combat and gathering skills. Plan your gold spending carefully.
:::

## Rebirth: Tidal Luck

Once Fishing reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Fishing to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Tidal Luck** passive.

## Tips

- Fishing is one of the most AFK-friendly skills — cast a line and wait.
- The high training cost means natural play past 250 is more gold-efficient than heavy trainer use.
- Use `/fishing` to open the fishing skill menu.
