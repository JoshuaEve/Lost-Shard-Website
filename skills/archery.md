# Archery

The **Archery** skill increases your effectiveness with bows. As the skill climbs, arrows deal more damage and you unlock the **Eagle Eye** rebirth perk.

## Leveling Archery

Archery experience is gained each time one of your arrows hits an entity (player or mob). Every hit counts, so sustained ranged combat is the fastest way to level.

## Damage Bonus

Archery adds bonus damage to bow attacks based on skill tier:

| Skill Range | Bonus Damage |
|-------------|-------------|
| 0 – 249 | +0 |
| 250 – 499 | +1 |
| 500 – 749 | +2 |
| 750 – 999 | +3 |
| 1000 | +4 |

## Training

Archery can be trained at a trainer NPC up to **level 250**:

- **Training cost:** 4 gold per level
- **Training cap:** 250

## Rebirth: Eagle Eye

Once Archery reaches **1000**, you can rebirth the skill. Rebirthing resets Archery to 0 and permanently unlocks the **Eagle Eye** passive, which carries through all future skill progressions.

## Tips

- Bows are most effective at range — use elevation and terrain to stay out of melee range.
- Combine Archery with [Taming](/skills/taming) to have your wolves tank while you shoot from range.
- Archery pairs well with [Magery](/skills/magery): snare enemies with Slow Field, then unload arrows.
