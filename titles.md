# Titles

Titles are **cosmetic prefixes** displayed before your name in chat and on the scoreboard. They let you show off your achievements and personalize how you appear to other players.

## Selecting a Title

Open the title selection GUI with:

```
/title
```

Browse your available titles and click one to equip it. Only titles that have been unlocked for your account are selectable.

## Unlocking Titles

Titles are granted by admins. If you believe you've earned a title through gameplay achievements or events, contact a staff member.

## Admin Title Management

Admins can open the title management GUI for any player:

```
/title admin <player>
```

This allows staff to grant or revoke titles on a per-player basis.

## Tips

- Titles are purely cosmetic and have no gameplay impact.
- Equipping an appropriate title can signal your role on the server (e.g., clan leader, veteran player, event winner).
- Check with staff about what titles are available and how to earn them.
